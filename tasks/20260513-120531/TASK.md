- STATUS: OPEN

## Goal

Define the scope and backlog for the next sprint (June 2026) based on the
current state of the project.

## Scope

### In
- Review the current state of the project, open issues, and pending tasks.
- Identify key priorities for the next sprint, focusing on critical features,
    bug fixes, and technical debt.
- Create a prioritized backlog of tasks for the sprint, ensuring clear
    acceptance criteria and estimates for each task.
- Plan for any necessary research or spikes to address unknowns in upcoming
    work.

### Out
- Execution of the sprint tasks (covered in future sprints).
- Retrospective and sprint review (covered in future sprints).

## Acceptance criteria
- A clear and prioritized backlog of tasks for the June 2026 sprint exists in

- Each task has well-defined acceptance criteria.
